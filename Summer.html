SUMMER 2017

I traveled to three places this summer: Miami, Punta Cana, and Canada.

One of my trips was to Punta Cana, in the Dominican Republic. This has been one of my favorite trips so far. It was just seven days of no responsibilites. It was a pretty special trip.

Another trip was to Toronto, Ontario. I've always wanted to go to Canada so I was pretty excited. My boyfriend lives there too, so finding places to go was pretty stree-free.

My longest trip was to Miami this July. I went for nine days with my best friends Joao and David. His sister lived down there too, so they showed us around. It was never boring and I wish I was back at the beach.
