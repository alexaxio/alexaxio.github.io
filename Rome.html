Travels

I have traveled to many places, including Greece, Bermuda, Ukraine, and Florida. One of my favorite places, however, was when I visited Italy and Spain on a school trip my junior year. It was over April vacation and I don't think that I will ever have an April vacation that compares. I miss the gelato in Italy and the churros in Barcelona.

I threw a quarter into the Trevi Fountain when in Rome.

I spend three days in Capri. It's waiting for my return.

In my opinion, the Blue Grotto in Anacapri should be one of the seven wonders. It's too breathtaking.

I went swimming in the oceans of Italy. I can die happy.

The most beautiful architecture lies in Barcelona.
