MY DOG DUNKIN

This is my dog Dunkin. He was named after the coffee store everyone in New England knows and loves, Dunkin Donuts. He is only about a year old, and is just a puff ball of craziness.

He is my favorite dog in the whole wide world. He liked to escape the house a lot, and when he does, he runs in circles around the neighborhood. When we find him he looks so mischievous. That is how I know he is my dog.
